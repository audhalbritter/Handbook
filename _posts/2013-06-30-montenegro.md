I spent some days in Montenegro in Virpazar near Skadar Lake. I went hiking, with a group of Biologist. Everybody was interested in some other flower, bird, snake or anything made by nature. Therefore, some people of the group got lost after 30 min wondering of looking for giant spiders. After ages the group was complete again and we could start climbing the mountain in 35Â°C.

Everyday we went swimming in the lake. The water was very warm but still refreshing.

Montenegro is a beautiful country with very friendly people and worth a visit. Unfortunately it was so hot that I only managed to make a few good shots.
